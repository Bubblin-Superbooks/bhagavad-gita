Dance to what tune HE will. With all thy soul
Trust Him, and take Him for thy succour, Prince! So—only so, Arjuna!—shalt thou gain—
By grace of Him—the uttermost repose,
The Eternal Place!

Thus hath been opened thee
This Truth of Truths, the Mystery more hid
Than any secret mystery. Meditate! And—as thou wilt—then act!

Nay! but once more
Take My last word, My utmost meaning have! Precious thou art to Me; right well-beloved! Listen! I tell thee for thy comfort this.
Give Me thy heart! adore Me! serve Me! cling
In faith and love and reverence to Me! So shalt thou come to Me! I promise true,
For thou art sweet to Me!
