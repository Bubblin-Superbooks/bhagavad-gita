Chapter Xviii

``` Arjuna.
Fain would I better know, Thou Glorious One! The very truth—Heart's Lord!—of Sannyas,
Abstention; and enunciation, Lord! Tyaga; and what separates these twain!

Krishna.
The poets rightly teach that Sannyas
Is the foregoing of all acts which spring
Out of desire; and their wisest say
Tyaga is renouncing fruit of acts.
